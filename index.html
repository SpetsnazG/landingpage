Multi-Wallet Bundling on Bullz
With Bullz, you can create multiple sub-wallets, allowing you to purchase any coin on the Solana network in a single transaction across multiple wallets. This bundling feature makes multi-wallet management seamless and efficient.
Fastest Transactions
Bullz operates its own infrastructure nodes, ensuring that your transactions are executed with minimal fees and at the fastest possible speed. This robust setup significantly reduces the likelihood of transaction failures.
Lowest Fees
Bullz charges no additional fees beyond standard protocol fees for NFT holders and premium members, enabling you to perform various transactions at minimal cost.
Anti-MEV Protection
Bullz leverages the Jito protocol to safeguard your funds from Miner Extractable Value (MEV) exploits. This extra layer of protection helps to secure your assets and maintain transaction integrity.
Fully Customizable
All transactions on the platform, including settings like slippage and priority fees, are entirely up to the user's preferences. Bullz hands over full control to the users for these adjustments.
